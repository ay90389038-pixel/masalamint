MASALA MINT

Fresh Taste, Trusted Hygiene

Standardizing Rural Street Food & Empowering Local Communities

Vision & Mission

"To become India's most trusted rural street food brand while empowering local entrepreneurs."

Our Mission

  • Serve completely hygienic food at reasonable rates.
  • Standardize roadside vending systems in villages.
  • Generate scalable rural employment opportunities.
  • Expand methodically, village by village.

Core Pillars

Hygiene • Quality • Affordability • Trust • Employment • Sustainability

By replacing ad-hoc unbranded operations with organized structures, we unlock local micro-entrepreneurship.

The Challenge in Rural Food Markets

01. Critical Hygiene Deficit

Most village food stalls completely lack hygiene infrastructure leading to major health contamination risks.

02. Zero Consistency

No standard recipes exist. Rural consumers experience wildly fluctuating quality and unpredictable taste profiles.

03. Disconnected Talent

Villagers possess excellent cooking skills but lack capital, business guidance, marketing outreach, and branding.

04. Lack of Trusted Options

Villages do not feature organized food brands. Residents depend completely on unverified seasonal vendors.

The Masala Mint Solution

A Standardized, Micro-Franchise Food Cart System

What Vendors Get

A branded, optimized food cart, rigorous culinary & operations training, supply chains, and structured marketing support.

What Customers Get

Guaranteed health-safe food, exceptionally affordable price structures, and great taste consistency.

Standard Operating Procedures

Strict operational tracking metrics: mandatory hairnets, uniform aprons, gloves, and purified clean water usage.

Strategic Pilot Rollout

Initial Launch Location

Village Cluster: Dulehra, Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh

Target Base: Population of ~1,300 + bordering high-density villages.

Target Demographics: Students, local farmers, daily wage workers, commuting workforce, and rural families (Ages 15–50).

Flagship Focus Strategy

Instead of deploying heavily across markets simultaneously, we will establish one flagship outlet to achieve:

  • Minimal initial operational capital risk
  • High-precision quality & process feedback loops
  • Strong localized community trust metrics

Business & Revenue Architecture

Core Offerings

Food & Beverage Items: Burgers, Momos, Spring Rolls, French Fries, Sandwiches, Cold Coffee, Mint Lemonade, and Traditional Teas.

Diverse Revenue Streams

  • Direct Counter Food Sales (Primary)
  • Micro-Franchise Setup & Brand Licensing fees (Future)
  • Corporate/Bulk Catering Options
  • Localized Village Festival & Mela Stalls
Our Competitive Edge (USP): Uncompromising hygiene, structural branding identity, predictable localized taste profiles, and modern integrated UPI digital payment setups.

Social Impact & Sustainability

Community Transformation

  • Direct local employment infrastructure creation.
  • Vocational business and hygiene management skill loops.
  • Fostering local micro-economies by prioritizing regional suppliers.
  • Empowerment opportunities for rural women vendor tracks.

Eco-Responsibility Protocols

  • 100% committed to zero open littering practices.
  • Strict usage of paper and biodegradable leaf-based packaging.
  • Active localized waste segregation programs.
  • Proactive minimization of single-use kitchen plastics.
