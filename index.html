How Masala Mint Works

A Simple, Scalable Flow

We believe in methodical expansion that guarantees quality at every doorstep. This is how we bring Masala Mint to you:

  1. Select village
  2. Recruit local vendor
  3. Train vendor
  4. Set up branded cart
  5. Daily quality checks
  6. Expand to nearby villages

Experience the Magic

Our Vision

Roadside food in villages often lacks consistency and hygiene. We are solving this. **Masala Mint** brings standardized recipes, professional food carts, and certified safety protocols to rural communities.

We operate via branded food carts, replacing unbranded, irregular setups with reliable, clean micro-franchises. Our dream is to become India's most trusted rural street food brand.

Hygiene First

We build trust through visibility. We operate under strict Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) developed to provide complete peace of mind to our customers.

  • Mandatory Hair Caps
  • Professional Uniform Aprons
  • Daily Certified Gloves
  • Purified Drinking Water
  • Covered Food Storage
  • Daily Deep Cleaning
